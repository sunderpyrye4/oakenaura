Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

Note: This Refund Policy applies to services provided by Oaken & Aura. Product refunds are handled by the respective manufacturers or suppliers.

1. Initial Consultation Fees

The initial consultation fee is refundable under the following conditions:

  • Full Refund: If you cancel at least 48 hours before the scheduled consultation.
  • Partial Refund (50%): If you cancel with less than 48 hours notice but before the consultation begins.
  • No Refund: If you cancel after the consultation has begun or fail to attend without notice.

You may also choose to reschedule your consultation instead of canceling, subject to availability.

2. Design Package Refunds

For design packages and ongoing projects, refunds are calculated based on work completed:

  • Before Work Commences: 100% refund of any deposits paid.
  • After Initial Concept Presentation: 50% refund of the total package price, minus the value of work completed.
  • After Final Design Delivery: No refund available, as all design work has been completed.

3. Hourly Consultation Refunds

For hourly consultation services:

  • Unused prepaid hours are refundable at any time.
  • Used hours are non-refundable.
  • Refunds will be prorated based on the hourly rate.

4. Product Purchases

Products purchased through Oaken & Aura (furniture, accessories, materials) are subject to the return policies of the individual manufacturers and suppliers. We will assist with returns and exchanges when possible, but final approval rests with the supplier.

Common product return conditions include:

  • Returns must be initiated within the supplier's specified timeframe (typically 14-30 days)
  • Products must be in original condition with all packaging
  • Custom or special order items are typically non-returnable
  • Shipping costs for returns are usually the responsibility of the client

5. Processing Refunds

Refund requests must be submitted in writing to accounts@oakenaura.com.

Once approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original method of payment. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your financial institution.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Custom design work that has been delivered and approved
  • Special order or custom-manufactured items
  • Services that have been fully rendered
  • Retainers for services that have commenced

7. Exceptions and Discretionary Refunds

In exceptional circumstances, we may offer refunds or service credits at our discretion, including:

  • Documented service failure on our part
  • Extenuating circumstances (illness, relocation, etc.) with proper documentation
  • Mutual agreement to terminate services for reasons outside the scope of this policy

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Date of service or purchase
  • Invoice or receipt number
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Any supporting documentation
