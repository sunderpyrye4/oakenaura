About Oaken & Aura

Founded in 2010, Oaken & Aura has established itself as a premier home décor consultancy serving clients across the United States. Our philosophy centers on creating harmonious living spaces that balance beauty, comfort, and personal expression.

With a team of 15 dedicated designers, we've transformed over 500 homes, from cozy apartments to sprawling estates. Our approach combines timeless design principles with contemporary trends to deliver spaces that stand the test of time.

We believe your home should be a sanctuary that reflects your personality while providing functional elegance for everyday living.
